Sikki Art is a traditional craft from Bihar, India, where artisans use golden-hued sikki grass to create intricate handmade items.
The grass is braided and shaped into decorative figures, baskets, jewelry, and utility objects, often embellished with vibrant natural dyes.
This eco-friendly art form reflects the creativity and cultural heritage of rural communities, transforming simple materials into exquisite and sustainable works of art.
