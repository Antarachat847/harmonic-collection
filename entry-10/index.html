GALलERY

Rajasthani Miniature Painting is a traditional art form from Rajasthan, India, known for its intricate details,
vibrant colors, and delicate brushwork. Emerging in the 16th century, these paintings often depict royal courts, epic tales, nature,
and religious themes. Artists use natural dyes and fine brushes to create intricate designs on paper, fabric, or marble.
Each piece reflects a blend of Mughal and local Rajput styles, showcasing Rajasthan’s rich cultural heritage and artistic finesse.
