Pattachitra is a traditional form of scroll painting that originates from the eastern regions of India, particularly Odisha and West Bengal.
Known for its intricate detailing and vibrant use of natural colors, Pattachitra often depicts mythological stories, folklore, and religious themes.
The art form is celebrated for its bold lines and rich narratives,making each piece a testament to the skill and cultural heritage of its artisans.
