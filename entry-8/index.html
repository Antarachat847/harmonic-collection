Meenakari is a traditional Indian art of enameling metals with vibrant intricate designs.
Originating from Persia and flourishing in Rajasthan, this craft involves fusing colorful enamel onto gold,
silver, or copper surfaces to create exquisite jewelry, ornaments, and decorative items.
Known for its precision and artistry, Meenakari often features floral, geometric, and nature-inspired motifs.
It showcases a rich blend of craftsmanship and cultural heritage, making it a timeless treasure in Indian artistry.
