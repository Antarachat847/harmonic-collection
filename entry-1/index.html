SIKKI ART

Origin: India, Bihar ( Madhubani, Darbhanga and Sitamarhi )
Materials used: Sikki grass, thick iron needle, knife and scissors.
Product: jwellery, toys, dolls, baskets, furniture, sculpture, tools, etc

History

Sikki crafts have their origin from the states of Bihar and Uttar Pradesh of India.
It is the art of making craft items out of Sikki grass which is considered to be auspicious by the natives of Bihar.
These are weaved into various items like ornaments and certain daily used essential items.
In earlier times, the qualifications of women in these areas were judged by the amount of knowledge and skills they had in making Sikki crafts.
It is considered as one of the most beautiful arts made from beautiful golden grass which makes the crafts highly attractive.
